Module 2

3D Spatial Design

Three-dimensional explorations using Three.js, focusing on geometry, lighting, and spatial relationships

Student Sara Lin • Instructor Catherine Griffiths

This module explores three-dimensional design using Three.js and p5.js WEBGL, focusing on geometry, lighting, and spatial relationships. We examine how computational design can create immersive spatial experiences.

3D Experiments

Four core experiments demonstrating different aspects of 3D computational design

01

Orbit Controls & Navigation

Interactive 3D navigation system allowing users to explore spatial compositions through orbit controls. This demonstrates fundamental 3D interaction patterns and spatial awareness.

02

Primitive Geometry

Exploration of basic geometric forms and their spatial relationships. Demonstrates how simple shapes can create complex spatial compositions through arrangement and transformation.

03

Lighting & Materials

Advanced lighting systems and material properties in 3D space. Shows how light and material interaction can create depth, atmosphere, and visual hierarchy.

04

Spatial Canvas

Large-scale 3D environment demonstrating spatial composition and environmental design. Explores how computational design can create immersive spatial experiences.
