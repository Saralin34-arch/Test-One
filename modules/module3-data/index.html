Module 3

Data Visualization

Geospatial data visualization using D3.js, exploring air quality data and density contours

Student Sara Lin • Instructor Catherine Griffiths

This module explores geospatial data visualization using D3.js, focusing on air quality data analysis and density contour mapping. The visualization demonstrates how to process environmental data and create meaningful spatial representations.

Data Visualization Experiments

Two core experiments demonstrating different aspects of environmental data visualization

01

NYC Air Quality Contours

Interactive visualization of air quality data across New York City using density contours. The map shows pollution levels and air quality patterns, demonstrating how to process and visualize complex environmental datasets.

02

Environmental Relations Map

Interactive visualization presenting multiple environmental datasets on a single map, showing the relationships between urban trees, air quality measurements, and environmental complaints.
